About Me:

My name is Parul Charkole and I am a student attending the University of North Carolina at Charlotte. I have been making art work ever since I could remember and it is my main hobby and possible future profession. My art projects have won many awards through grade school along with awards for amateur artists. I have experience in water,oil,pastel,and acrylic painting, pencil base, and purely digital.

Accomplishments:

  • 1st Place for North Carolina Amateur Painting
  • Voted most realistic drawing for Mecklenburg County
  • Placing in the top 5 for a Digital Artwork

Why Im an artist

I am a artist because it is something I truly have a passion for and it has brought me happiness every since I was a child. It allows me to express myself and bring joy to others as I make their project ideas come true. It is a challenging hobby that I look forward to bringing up to a professional level.
